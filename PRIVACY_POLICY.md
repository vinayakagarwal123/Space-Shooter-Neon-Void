# Privacy Policy for Space Shooter : Neon Void

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-10

Welcome to Space Shooter : Neon Void! Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains what data the game uses and why.

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information ourselves.
*   No account is required to play.
*   We do not ask for your name, email, or any other personal identifiers.
*   We do not run our own servers — nothing you do in the game is sent to us.

## 2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This game shows ads served by **Google AdMob**. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process:
*   Your device's **Advertising ID**
*   IP address and coarse location derived from it
*   Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
*   Ad interaction data (which ads were shown, viewed, or tapped)

This data is collected and processed by Google under its own policies:
*   Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
*   How Google uses ad data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can reset or delete your Advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization at any time in your device settings under **Settings → Google → Ads**.

## 3. Local Data Storage

All your game progress (high scores, ship upgrades, missions, and settings) is stored **locally on your device**.
*   This data never leaves your phone.
*   We do not have access to your game progress.
*   If you delete the app or clear its storage, your progress is permanently deleted.

## 4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are served through Google AdMob in accordance with Google Play's Families and Ads policies.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

If we add features that change how data is handled, we will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date above.
