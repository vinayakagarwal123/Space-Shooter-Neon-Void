# Privacy Policy for Neon Void

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-15

Welcome to Neon Void! Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains our approach to data.

## 1. No Data Collection

Neon Void **does not collect, store, or share any personal information**. 
*   No account is required to play.
*   We do not ask for your name, email, or any other personal identifiers.
*   We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics.

## 2. Local Data Storage

All your game progress (high scores, ship upgrades, and settings) is stored **locally on your device**.
*   This data never leaves your phone.
*   We do not have access to your game progress.
*   If you delete the app or clear your phone's storage, your game progress will be lost because it is not saved on any server.

## 3. Optional Services

The game may use standard platform services provided by Google (like Google Play Games) for achievements or system-level features. These services are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. We do not store any information from these services on our own servers.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, Neon Void is safe for players of all ages.
